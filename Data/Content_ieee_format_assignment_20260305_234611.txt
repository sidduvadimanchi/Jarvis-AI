=== Main Concepts ===

The IEEE format has several key components that make it a unique and efficient citation style. Some of the main concepts include:

* **Title Page**: The title page should include the title of the paper, author's name, affiliation, and date.
* **Abstract**: A concise summary of the paper, typically 150-250 words.
* **Introduction**: An introduction to the topic, including background information and research questions.
* **Body**: The main content of the paper, including sections, subsections, and headings.
* **References**: A list of sources cited in the paper, formatted according to the IEEE style.
* **Citation Style**: The IEEE format uses a numerical citation style, where numbers are used to reference sources in the body of the paper.
